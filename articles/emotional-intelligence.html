08/19 — Emotional Intelligence

This class was about understanding emotional intelligence and why it is important in both personal and professional life. The lecturer explained the five main areas: self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills. We also learned how emotional intelligence helps us manage stress, build better relationships, and work effectively with others.
