08/05 — Job Interview

This session was about preparing for job interviews and understanding how to present ourselves in a professional way. The lecturer explained the types of interviews, common questions, and the importance of body language, confidence, and preparation. We also learned how to research a company before attending an interview to make a good impression.

Key points

  • Interview preparation
  • Professional presentation
  • Peer learning
