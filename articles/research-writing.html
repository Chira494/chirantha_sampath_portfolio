Research Writing

07/22 — Research Writing

In this class, we learned the basics of research writing and why it is important in academic work. The lecturer explained how to structure research papers, including the abstract, introduction, method, results, and conclusion. We also discussed how to use references correctly and avoid plagiarism by giving credit to original sources.

Key points

  • Learning research structure
  • Studying examples
  • Active participation
