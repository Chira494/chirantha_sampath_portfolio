CV Writing

06/17 — CV Writing

This session focused on how to create a professional CV. We learned about the main sections, such as personal details, education, work experience, skills, and achievements. The lecturer explained the difference between a basic CV and one tailored for a specific job. We also looked at common mistakes people make and how to keep a CV clear and easy to read.

Key points

  • Understanding CV structure
  • Reflecting on experiences
  • Peer learning
