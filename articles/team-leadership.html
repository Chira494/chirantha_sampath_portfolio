07/01 — Team Leadership

This lecture was about understanding what makes a good leader and how leadership is different from just managing people. We learned about different leadership styles, the importance of communication, and how leaders motivate and guide their teams. The lecturer also explained that leadership is not only for managers but something we all practice in group projects and daily life.

Key points

  • Qualities of a leader
  • Importance of direction
  • Personal reflection
