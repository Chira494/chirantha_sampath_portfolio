06/24 — Portfolio Writing

In this class, we learned about how to build a professional portfolio that shows our skills, achievements, and experiences. The lecturer explained that a portfolio is not only for creative fields but also useful for any student to present their work in an organized way. We looked at both physical and digital portfolios and discussed why keeping it updated is important.

Key points

  • Learning portfolio content
  • Planning digital portfolio
  • Identifying evidence
