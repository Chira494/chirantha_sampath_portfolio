08/26 — Dining Etiquettes

This session was about learning the proper manners and behavior to follow during formal meals. The lecturer explained the importance of dining etiquette in professional settings, such as business lunches or interviews. We covered topics like table settings, the correct use of cutlery, polite conversation, and respectful behavior at the table.

Key points

  • Dining etiquette basics
  • Practical skills
  • Professional importance
