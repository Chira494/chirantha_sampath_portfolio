About Me

I am Chirantha Sampath, a Software Engineering undergraduate at SLIIT City University (affiliated with the University of Bedfordshire, UK). I have strong technical skills in web development and database management, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL through academic projects and freelance work.

Beyond software, I am also a professional photographer with 3 years of experience in wedding, event, and wildlife photography, where I developed creativity, client-handling, and attention to detail.

I enjoy combining technical expertise with creative problem-solving, and I thrive in teamwork, leadership, and delivering high-quality results. My goal is to grow as a versatile IT professional who can apply both technical and creative skills to make a positive impact.
